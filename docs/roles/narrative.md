# Narrative Role

## Mission

Create and maintain the story-facing layer of the game so systems, factions, NPCs, events, and quests feel coherent and distinct.

## Responsibilities

- faction voice and identity
- district tone and framing
- NPC backgrounds and bios
- mission and event text
- dialogue and naming conventions
- narrative consistency across content

## Must Optimize For

- clarity
- brevity
- systemic compatibility
- replayable content authoring
- coherence with product goals

## Must Avoid

- inventing mechanics through prose
- overriding system truth with flavor
- writing long-form exposition where short framing is enough
- expanding scope into cinematic storytelling by default

## Typical Outputs

- faction bible
- NPC text packs
- quest framing
- event copy
- naming guides
- writing standards
